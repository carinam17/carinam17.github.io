## Carina Mata

**Instructional Designer · Mathematics Educator · EdTech Professional**

I design and deliver learning experiences that make complex ideas clear, usable, and engaging—especially for adult learners returning to technical or academic material. My experience spans instructional design, curriculum development, live teaching, and data-informed training.

I’ve worked across small-group courses, independent curriculum projects, and classroom instruction, with a consistent focus on structure, clarity, and learner outcomes.

---

## What I Do

- **Instructional Design & Curriculum Development**  
  Course frameworks, learning objectives, course materials, and modular instructional materials.

- **Teaching & Facilitation**  
  Live instruction in math and test preparation, with an emphasis on strategy, reflection, and confidence-building.

- **Educational Technology & Tools**  
  Content creation using Markdown, LaTeX, GitHub, and collaborative platforms to produce clear, reusable learning assets.
